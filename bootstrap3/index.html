Mowgli

Generating Happy Guests.

As your Sales Representative at Mowgli we will play an important role in the financial success of your privately owned hotel. By selling the entire range of amenities that your property has to offer, we will demonstrate all your customers the ways in which your hotel can best accommodate them.

Our main goal is to continually expand your company network of potential clients, while following up on the overall experience of past customers and partners in hopes of gaining repeat business.

As your Sales Representative we will network with companies, tour operators and travel agencies in a similar fashion, quoting prices and offering the best solutions for business or pleasure. Every day we will be out there for your clients, doing sales calls meeting with your clients personally, attending trade shows and by giving sales pitches. Additionally we can offer your hotel the most complete sales force including draw up of contracts, booking sales, verify reservations in writing, problem solve and analyse the requirements for potential clients.

We understand the pressure to sell and produce new customers often increases when a hotel experiences more direct competition. With more than 15 years of experience on the hotel and tourism business, our team have a wide vision on the changes in the hotel's facilities and amenities within your market. Additionally to Sales, we will also prepare monthly reports, keeping communication lines open within your property management.

With Mowgli your hotel will reach more than 10K companies, travel agencies and tour operators from the most important feeding markets in Europe, America and Africa.

We will also give you support on PR creating the necessary awareness to your propriety.

We are sure that knowledge of business can serve you as a valuable experience.

Thank you for your confidence, together we will increase occupancy, raise revenue and make your hotel a sales success!
